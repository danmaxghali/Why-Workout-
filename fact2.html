Exercise can prevent and treat diseases that occur naturally with age. By starting now you can build an amazing base, probably more than you think possible. Once you have the habits in place it will become a lot easier and you might even learn to enjoy it!

Examples of diseases that can be prevented with exercise:

  • Cardiovascular disease
  • Osteoporosis
  • Arthritis
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Neurodegenerative disorders: Parkinsons, dementia, alzheimers
  • Depression and anxiety

Sedentary adults can expect a 10-40% decrease in motion for each joint and 40% decrease in muscle mass as they age so by exercising throughout your life, you don't just extend the length, but also the quality.
