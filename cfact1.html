Affect of cardiovascular exercise on the heart

Moderate and intense cardiovascular exercise can cause your heart and circulatory system to adapt, which can allow you to live longer

It does this by:

  • Muscular growth of the heart - This makes it more efficient by doing the same amount of work for less effort, and subsequently lowers your resting heart rate.
  • Muscular growth of blood vessels - This allows them to carry more blood at a higher pressure, which makes exercise easier
