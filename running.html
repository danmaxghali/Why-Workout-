Running

Running is popular activity, and for good reason. Even the most minimal 5-10 minutes a day at less than 6mph is associated with reducing risk of death, especially when caused by cardiovascular disease. A study by Lee et al.(2014) found that if all the non-runners in their study became runners, cardiovascular disease deaths would drop by 25%!

To reduce death by such a percentage, it would involve consistent leisure running over a long period of time. This study ephasises that if you can just start by walking you will already begin to have an effect and create good habits.
